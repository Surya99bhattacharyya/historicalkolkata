ABOUT US

Learn more about our mission, vision, and values.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the rich cultural and historical heritage of Kolkata, providing a platform for education, awareness, and engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading source of historical knowledge about Kolkata, inspiring pride and appreciation for the city's past and its influence on the present and future.

Our Values

Commitment to authenticity, inclusivity, and community engagement. We value the diverse stories and contributions of all Kolkata residents, past and present.
