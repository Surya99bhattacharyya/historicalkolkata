Our Heritage

Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and as the birthplace of many great artists, writers, and social reformers. The city has a unique blend of colonial history, art, and literature, making it a vibrant hub of cultural activities.
